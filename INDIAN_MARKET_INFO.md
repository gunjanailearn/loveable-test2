### NIFTY 50
- **Full Name**: National Stock Exchange Fifty
- **What it is**: Index of top 50 companies listed on NSE
- **Base Year**: 1995 (Base value: 1000)
- **Represents**: ~65% of total market capitalization on NSE
- **Current Value**: ~21,894 points (example)

### SENSEX
- **Full Name**: Bombay Stock Exchange Sensitive Index
- **What it is**: Index of 30 well-established companies on BSE
- **Base Year**: 1978-79 (Base value: 100)
- **Oldest**: India's first stock market index
- **Current Value**: ~72,426 points (example)

### NIFTY BANK
- **Full Name**: NIFTY Bank Index
- **What it is**: Index of 12 most liquid banking stocks
- **Includes**: Public & private sector banks
- **Represents**: Banking sector performance
- **Current Value**: ~46,785 points (example)
